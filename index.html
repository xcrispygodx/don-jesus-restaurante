Our Story

A Taste of Mexico in Pleasanton

Don Jesus Restaurante is a beloved destination for delicious Mexican food at reasonable prices. Our menu features a wide variety of traditional Mexican dishes crafted with fresh ingredients and authentic recipes passed down through generations.

From sizzling fajitas and savory enchiladas to our famous huevos rancheros and fresh guacamole, every dish is prepared with love and care. We accommodate large groups, serve food quickly, and pride ourselves on great customer service.
